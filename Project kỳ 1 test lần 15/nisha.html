Nisha Katona partners with Haier

Meet Haier's new Brand Ambassador

“My mission is to enrich lives. I’m looking forward to continuing to do this by partnering with Haier, such a well-known and respected leader in its field”.

Haier, the No 1 Global Major Appliance brand for the 13th consecutive year*, is thrilled to welcome Nisha Katona MBE as its brand ambassador. It will see the popular tv chef and restauranteur promote Haier’s award-winning appliances whose mission has always been to place its customer at the heart of everything it creates.

This partnership will see the food expert and author promote Haier’s products across its portfolio of connected ovens, hobs, hoods, refrigeration, and wine fridges.

*Source: Euromonitor International Limited 2022.
